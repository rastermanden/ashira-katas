Kihon Kata Ichi

Overview

Kihon Kata Ichi is the foundational kata of Ashihara Karate. It introduces the core principles of movement, distance control (maai), and the circular evasion philosophy that defines Ashihara style. This kata establishes the attacker-defender relationship that runs through all Ashihara kata.

組手の形五

Sparring Form 5

Kumite no Kata Sono Go

Overview

Kumite no Kata Sono Go is the most advanced sparring form, featuring complex multi-kick combinations, hook kicks (kake-geri), and the integration of instep blocks with simultaneous counter kicks. The kata includes ura-nage against front kick entries with okuri ashi advancement.

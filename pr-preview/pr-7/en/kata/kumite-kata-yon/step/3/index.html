組手の型 四—Kumite no Kata — Sono Yon

Step 3 — Defence vs Right Front Kick & Right Punch

Step 3 of 10
← Previous StepStep 2 — Defence vs Right Low Roundhouse Kick
Next Step →Step 4 — Defence vs Left Low Roundhouse Kick

Attacker

The opponent attacks with a right front kick (migi mae-geri) followed by a right punch (migi tsuki).

Defender

Step back, receive with a left outside block combined with a stop-kick (hidari soto-uke + stopping), counter with a right mid-level roundhouse kick (migi chudan mawashi-geri), break the opponent down (hikikuzushi), and finish with another right mid-level roundhouse kick (migi chudan mawashi-geri) — kicked toward the face but at mid height.

Technical Notes

Source: "3. Migi mae-geri, migi tsuki: Step back, hidari soto-uke + stopping, migi chudan mawashi-geri, hikikuzushi, migi chudan mawashi-geri. (kick to the face but chudan height)" Stepping back creates distance for the soto-uke and stopping to cover both the kick and the punch; the hikikuzushi breaks the balance between the two chudan mawashi-geri.

Jissen no Kata — Sono Ichi

Overview

Jissen no Kata — Sono Ichi is the combat ("real fight") form of Ashihara Karate, performed solo against an imagined opponent. It works at close range, using inside blocks, boxing-style hooks and uppercuts, elbow strikes and close-range counters against punches and kicks. The form closes with a punch and a downward elbow strike before returning to the fighting stance.
